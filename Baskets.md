Curated baskets organized by NHANES 2021-2023 meal archetypes.

Archetype definitions are derived from the WWEIA dietary recall analysis in
`analysis/nhanes/nhanes_meal_archetypes.html`.  Each archetype is mapped to
grocery product patterns via NiQ supercategory coverage (see the 20-category
scorecard in the Rmd source).  The broad "other breakfast/lunch/dinner/snack"
buckets are excluded because they are large but diagnostically weak.
